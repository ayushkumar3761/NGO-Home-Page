About Our Organization

Our Mission

Founded in 2010, Green Root Initiative is dedicated to creating sustainable solutions for communities in need. Our mission is to empower individuals through education, healthcare access, and community development programs.

Our Approach

We believe in working with local communities to identify their real needs and develop sustainable solutions. Our team operates with transparency, accountability, and a commitment to measurable impact.

  • 100% of donations go directly to programs
  • Locally-led initiatives for sustainable impact
  • Transparent reporting and accountability
